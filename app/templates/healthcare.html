Healthcare Technology

Helping people with technology has always been an interest of mine, but when I helped a family understood their daughter's disease with a virtual reality application I built (video below) something in me changed. The idea that technology can help us understand what's inside us, improve peoples' access to care, and improve clinical outcomes is what excites me when I wake up. The mountains of underused and ignored, potentially life-saving data is what drives me to research novel ways to make data-driven decisions. My current work preserves the 3-dimensionality of data produced during cardiac catheterization procedures to better understand how virtual reality and mixed reality can facilitate minimally invasive therapies (videos below). In the near future, I hope to build on this work to understand how self driving catheters can address access to care problems.
