HMD Procedural Guidance Systems

Endovascular procedures are minimally invasive treatments that are rapidly improving vascular care. Unfortunately, many of these procedures are expensive, challenging and geographically confined. Head mounted displays (HMDs) offer new opportunities to improve safety, increase portability and decrease costs. My research explores these opportunities in an effort to improve clinical outcomes and increase who has access to care. Rather than viewing on a flat screen, HMDs allow us to interact with medical data in the form of the object the data represents. This allows us to understand it quicker and more accurately, which influences our decision making.
